# ArcaneFlux Gameplay Guide

## Overview
ArcaneFlux is a fast-paced futuristic fantasy arena game where you battle waves of enemies using magical spells with chaotic properties. Each wave you survive grants you powerful mutations that transform your playstyle.

## Controls
- **WASD** - Move your character
- **Mouse** - Aim spells
- **Left Click** - Cast spell

## Enemy Types

### Basic (Red)
- Standard melee enemies
- Chase the player directly
- Evolve behavior patterns over time

### Shooter (Orange)
- Appears from Wave 4+
- Fires projectiles at the player
- Slower but dangerous at range

### Tank (Purple)
- Appears from Wave 6+
- Double health and larger size
- Slow but resilient

### Fast (Teal)
- Appears from Wave 8+
- Much faster movement
- Lower health but hard to hit

## Arena Hazards

### Rotating Floor
- The entire arena slowly rotates
- Rotation speed increases with wave number
- Adds disorientation to combat

### Exploding Tiles
- Random floor tiles explode
- Creates visual chaos
- Increases in frequency with waves

### Moving Hazards
- Red danger zones appear from Wave 3+
- Move around the arena
- Deal contact damage to player

## Mutation System
After clearing each wave, choose from 3 random mutations:

### Offensive Mutations
- **Rapid Fire** - 50% faster spell cooldown
- **Glass Cannon** - Double damage, -20 max health
- **Explosive Finale** - All spells explode on impact
- **Volatile Magic** - 30% backfire chance, 2.5x damage

### Defensive Mutations
- **Shield Burst** - +30 max health, heal 20
- **Speed Demon** - +50% movement speed
- **Regeneration** - Slowly heal over time

### Spell Mutations
- **Chaos Storm** - 30% chance spells split on cast
- **Ricochet Master** - All spells bounce twice more
- **Spell Size** - +50% larger projectiles

## Strategy Tips

1. **Keep Moving** - Standing still is death. Constantly circle-strafe enemies.

2. **Use Walls** - Ricochet spells can hit enemies from unexpected angles.

3. **Crowd Control** - Save explosive spells for when enemies cluster.

4. **Mutation Synergy** - Combine mutations for powerful effects:
   - Rapid Fire + Chaos Storm = Spell spam
   - Explosive Finale + Spell Size = Massive AoE
   - Ricochet Master + Volatile Magic = Chaos everywhere

5. **Enemy Priority** - Kill shooters first, they're the most dangerous.

6. **Learn Behaviors** - Enemies switch between chase, circle, and dodge. Predict their movements.

7. **Arena Awareness** - Keep track of hazards and use arena rotation to your advantage.

## Visual Indicators
- **Player glow** - Cyan when normal, white + flashing when invulnerable
- **Health bar** - Green above 50%, yellow above 25%, red below
- **Spell trails** - Colored trails show spell paths
- **Particles** - Explosions and impacts create particle effects
- **Enemy health bars** - Shows remaining health above each enemy

## Advanced Techniques

### Kiting
Move in large circles around the arena to keep distance from melee enemies while focusing fire on shooters.

### Spell Leading
Aim where enemies will be, not where they are. Predict their movement patterns.

### Wall Bouncing
Position yourself so ricochet spells bounce from walls into enemy groups.

### Mutation Stacking
Some mutations can be selected multiple times (Rapid Fire, Speed Demon, Shield Burst) for compounding effects.

### Invulnerability Frames
You have brief invulnerability after taking damage. Use it to escape dangerous situations.
